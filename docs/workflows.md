# Best Starting Workflows

Use this page when you want to browse the repo by job-to-be-done instead of by plugin name.

---

## 1. Discovery and validation

**Use when:** you are exploring a new idea or debugging weak user outcomes

```text
/discover → /lean-validation → /setup-metrics
```

---

## 2. Product strategy and planning

**Use when:** you need product direction, pricing, or business model clarity

```text
/strategy → /business-model → /pricing → /challenge
```

---

## 3. Delivery and execution

**Use when:** you need a PRD, backlog, test plan, and sprint-ready scope

```text
/write-prd → /write-stories → /test-scenarios → /sprint
```

---

## 4. Go-to-market

**Use when:** you are planning a launch and need messaging plus channel choices

```text
/plan-launch → /growth-strategy → /marketing-plan → /battlecard
```

---

## 5. AI feature launch

**Use when:** your feature depends on model quality, trust, and safety

```text
/ai-feature-launch → /responsible-ai-review → /ai-metrics
```

---

## 6. PM-builder workflow

**Use when:** you are using AI coding tools to prototype or ship something quickly

```text
/plan-prototype → /vibe-spec → /tech-decision → /review-code → /deploy-check
```

---

## 7. Team onboarding and PM upskilling

**Use when:** you want a structured PM learning path

```text
/course → /learn-foundations → /learn-execution → /learn-capstone
```

---

## By product type

| Product type | Good first workflows |
|---|---|
| B2B SaaS | discovery, execution, GTM |
| Consumer product | discovery, analytics, GTM |
| Platform / internal product | strategy, execution, stakeholder management |
| AI product | AI feature launch, analytics, strategy |
| New PM team | PM learning, PM Core starter pack |
