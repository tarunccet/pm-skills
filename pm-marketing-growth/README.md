# pm-marketing-growth

Product marketing and growth skills: marketing ideas, product naming, and positioning. Value proposition statements are now part of the unified `value-proposition` skill in pm-product-strategy. North Star metrics are now part of the unified `product-metrics` skill in pm-data-analytics.

## Skills (3)

- **marketing-ideas** — Generate 5 creative, cost-effective marketing ideas with channels, messaging, and engagement rationale.
- **positioning-ideas** — Brainstorm product positioning ideas differentiated from competitors.
- **product-name** — Brainstorm 5 unique, memorable product names with rationale aligned to brand values and target audience.

## Commands (2)

- `/pm-marketing-growth:market-product` — Brainstorm marketing ideas, positioning, and product names — creative marketing toolkit.
- `/pm-marketing-growth:north-star` — Define your North Star Metric and supporting input metrics — classify the business game and validate against best practices. (Uses the `product-metrics` skill in pm-data-analytics.)
