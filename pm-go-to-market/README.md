# pm-go-to-market

Go-to-market skills for PMs: GTM strategy, growth loops, GTM motions, ideal customer profiles, competitive battlecards, marketing ideas, positioning, and product naming.

## Skills (9)

- **growth-loops** — Identify growth loops (flywheels) for sustainable traction.
- **gtm-motions** — Identify the best GTM motions and tools.
- **gtm-strategy** — Create a go-to-market strategy for a product launch covering marketing channels, messaging, success metrics, and launch plan.
- **ideal-customer-profile** — Identify the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) from research data with demographics, behaviors, JTBD, and needs.
- **competitive-battlecard** — Sales-ready battlecard with positioning, feature comparison, objection handling, and win strategies.
- **beachhead-segment** — Identify the first beachhead market segment for focused initial go-to-market.
- **marketing-ideas** — Generate creative, cost-effective marketing campaign ideas with channels and messaging.
- **positioning-ideas** — Develop product positioning differentiated from competitors.
- **product-name** — Brainstorm product names aligned to brand values and target audience.

## Commands (4)

- `/pm-go-to-market:battlecard` — Create a sales-ready competitive battlecard — positioning, feature comparison, objection handling, and win strategies.
- `/pm-go-to-market:growth-strategy` — Design sustainable growth mechanisms — growth loops and GTM motions for product-led and sales-led strategies.
- `/pm-go-to-market:plan-launch` — Create a full go-to-market strategy — ICP, messaging, channels, and launch plan.
- `/pm-go-to-market:marketing-plan` — Brainstorm marketing ideas, positioning, value props, and product names — creative marketing toolkit.
