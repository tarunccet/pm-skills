| I want to… | Use this skill | Not this one |

| Define strategic market segments | `user-segmentation` in market mode (market-research) | `research-personas` (market-research) |
| Pick a beachhead launch segment | `user-segmentation` in beachhead mode (market-research) | `research-personas` (market-research) |
